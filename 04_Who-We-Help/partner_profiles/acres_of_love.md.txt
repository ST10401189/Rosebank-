# Partner Profile

Acres of Love

Mission: To rescue and nurture children who have been abandoned or orphaned in South Africa.

Overview:
Founded in 1998, Acres of Love operates over 30 Forever Homes across Johannesburg, Benoni, and Cape Town. Each home provides family-style residential care for vulnerable children—ensuring stability, education, healthcare, and emotional support.

Why TOL partners with them:
* Proven track record of long-term childcare.
* Transparent reporting and measurable impact.
* Strong alignment with TOL’s focus on sustainable transformation.
