Agapé Family Ministries

Mission: To provide holistic care for disabled, abandoned, and orphaned children in the Western Cape.

Overview:
Located near Grabouw, Agapé serves children with special needs and disabilities, many of whom were abandoned at hospitals. The ministry offers residential family care, schooling support, and therapy programs, fostering dignity and hope.

Why TOL partners with them:

- Deep commitment to vulnerable children with special needs.

- Emphasis on both practical care and spiritual growth.

- Long-term sustainability and trusted governance.
