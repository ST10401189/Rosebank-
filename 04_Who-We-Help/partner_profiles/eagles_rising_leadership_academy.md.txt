Eagles Rising Leadership Academy

Mission: To raise the next generation of South African leaders through Christian discipleship and education.

Overview:
Eagles Rising is a residential academy that takes young adults through intensive leadership development, vocational training, and faith-based mentorship. Their programs emphasize character, leadership, and community transformation.

Why TOL partners with them:

- Clear focus on leadership pipeline for South Africa’s future.

- Structured, immersive programs with proven alumni success.

- Strong Christian ethos in line with TOL’s founding values.
